## Executive Summary

Zzyra is being developed as a Web3 automation platform that will enable users to create, deploy, and manage automated workflows for blockchain and DeFi operations. The platform will combine visual workflow building with AI-assisted automation to democratize access to complex Web3 operations.

<Info>
  **Vision**: Zzyra aims to bridge the gap between traditional automation and Web3 operations,
  providing a no-code solution for complex blockchain workflows while building towards 
  enterprise-grade security and scalability.
</Info>
