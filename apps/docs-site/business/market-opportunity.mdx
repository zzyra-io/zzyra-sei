## The Hybrid Automation Market Opportunity

Zzyra addresses a massive and rapidly growing market opportunity at the intersection of Web3 automation, enterprise process automation, and AI-powered workflow optimization. The convergence of these three markets creates a unique opportunity for a platform that can bridge traditional business operations with emerging blockchain technologies.

<Info>
  The total addressable market (TAM) for hybrid automation spans multiple
  multi-billion dollar markets, with Zzyra positioned to capture significant
  share through its unique AI-native approach and comprehensive integration
  capabilities.
</Info>

- **Protocol Teams & DAOs**: 5K+ active protocols and organizations

**Focus**: Core Web3 automation capabilities
**Metrics**: User acquisition, workflow creation, retention

**Go-to-Market**:

- Community-driven growth in DeFi/NFT communities
- Content marketing and educational resources
- Influencer partnerships in Web3 space
- Freemium model with premium features

### Phase 2: Enterprise Expansion (Months 13-24)

**Target**: Portfolio managers and mid-market enterprises
