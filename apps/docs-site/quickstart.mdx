## Step 1: Create Your First Workflow

### Using Natural Language (Recommended for Beginners)

<Steps>
  <Step title='Navigate to AI Workflow Generator'>
    Click the **"Create with AI"** button on your dashboard to open the
    intelligent workflow generator.
  </Step>

{" "}
<Step title='Describe Your Automation Goal'>
  Type your goal in plain English: ``` "Monitor my DeFi portfolio and send me a
  Slack notification when any token drops more than 10% in a day, but only
  during business hours to avoid spam" ```
</Step>

{" "}
<Step title='Review AI-Generated Workflow'>
  Zzyra's AI will analyze your request and generate a complete workflow with: -
  Portfolio monitoring blocks - Price change detection logic - Time-based
  filtering - Slack notification integration
</Step>

  <Step title='Customize Settings'>
    Fine-tune the generated workflow: - Adjust percentage thresholds - Select
    specific tokens to monitor - Set business hours for your timezone -
    Configure notification preferences
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Using Visual Builder (For Advanced Users)

<Steps>
  <Step title='Open Workflow Builder'>
    Click **"Create Workflow"** and select **"Visual Builder"** for
    drag-and-drop workflow creation.
  </Step>

{" "}
<Step title='Add Portfolio Monitor Block'>
  Drag the **"Portfolio Monitor"** block from the Web3 category to the canvas.
  Configure: - Connected wallets to monitor - Refresh interval (recommended: 5
  minutes) - Chains to include (Ethereum, Polygon, etc.)
</Step>

{" "}
<Step title='Add Price Change Detection'>
  Connect a **"Price Change Detector"** block: - Set threshold: 10% decrease -
  Time window: 24 hours - Comparison method: Percentage change
</Step>

{" "}
<Step title='Add Time Filter'>
  Include a **"Time Window Filter"** block: - Business hours: 9 AM - 6 PM -
  Timezone: Your local timezone - Weekdays only: Optional
</Step>

  <Step title='Add Notification Block'>
    Connect a **"Slack Notification"** block: - Channel: #crypto-alerts -
    Message template: AI-generated with token details - Include portfolio impact
    analysis
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Configure Integrations

### Connect Your DeFi Portfolio

<Tabs>
  <Tab title='Wallet Connection'>
    - Click **"Connect Wallet"** in the Portfolio Monitor block - Select your
    wallet provider (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.) - Grant read-only
    permissions - Zzyra will automatically discover your token holdings
  </Tab>

{" "}
<Tab title='Multi-Chain Setup'>
  - Enable monitoring for multiple chains - Zzyra automatically detects assets
  across: - Ethereum Mainnet - Polygon - Arbitrum - Optimism - Base (and more)
</Tab>

  <Tab title='Custom Tokens'>
    - Add specific token contracts for monitoring - Zzyra validates contracts and
    fetches metadata - Set individual notification thresholds per token
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 3: Test Your Workflow

### Simulation Mode

Before going live, test your workflow safely:

<Steps>
  <Step title='Enable Test Mode'>
    Toggle **"Simulation Mode"** in your workflow settings to test without real
    transactions or notifications.
  </Step>

{" "}
<Step title='Run Historical Test'>
  Use the **"Historical Simulation"** feature to see how your workflow would
  have performed with past market data.
</Step>

{" "}
<Step title='Validate Configuration'>
  Zzyra's AI will analyze your workflow and suggest optimizations: - Threshold
  adjustments based on volatility - Notification timing optimization -
  Additional monitoring suggestions
</Step>

  <Step title='Review Test Results'>
    Examine the test execution log to ensure everything works as expected before
    enabling live mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title='Wallet Connection Issues' icon='wallet'>
    - Ensure wallet is unlocked and connected to the correct network - Check
    browser permissions for the wallet extension - Try connecting through
    WalletConnect if MetaMask fails
  </Accordion>

{" "}
<Accordion title='Workflow Not Triggering' icon='exclamation-triangle'>
  - Verify all required integrations are properly connected - Check that trigger
  conditions are realistic for current market conditions - Review execution logs
  for error messages
</Accordion>

  <Accordion title='Notification Delivery Issues' icon='bell'>
    - Confirm notification channels are properly configured - Check spam folders
    for email notifications - Verify Slack/Discord bot permissions
  </Accordion>
