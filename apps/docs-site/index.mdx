Zzyra is an AI-assisted workflow automation platform in active development, designed to bridge Web3 ecosystems and enterprise systems. The platform aims to enable organizations to automate complex multi-chain operations, optimize DeFi strategies, and integrate blockchain functionalities within enterprise workflows.

## Key Capabilities

    sequential thinking capabilities, and 11+ MCP tool integrations for intelligent
    workflow automation and decision making.

  <Accordion title='Blockchain & DeFi' icon='bitcoin'>

## Use Cases

Zzyra aims to enable automation across diverse scenarios:

- **DeFi Optimization**: Multi-protocol yield farming, liquidity management, and arbitrage strategies (In Development)
- **AI-Enhanced Workflows**: Intelligent workflow generation and optimization using natural language (Core Features Implemented)
- **Sei Network Integration**: Native Sei blockchain operations and smart contract interactions (Implemented)
- **Enterprise Automation**: Cross-platform business process automation and integration (Planned)
- **Cross-Chain Operations**: Multi-blockchain workflow coordination and asset management (Roadmap)
